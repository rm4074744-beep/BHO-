Content Regulation & Integrity
Content Regulation & Anti-Harassmentexpand_more
MingleJoys strictly regulates all uploaded content to prevent the spread of hate speech, harassment, and cyberbullying. We do not allow any content that targets individuals or groups with the intent to intimidate or silence them. Sharing indecent or obscene materials that violate communal standards is also strictly prohibited. Our regulation system is designed to identify and remove harmful interactions, ensuring that every user can engage without fear.
Combating Fake News & Misinformationexpand_more
Maintaining the truth is essential for a healthy community. We require users to share only verified and accurate information. Intentionally spreading fake news, rumors, or unverified reports that could cause panic or misinformation is a violation of our standards. Users are encouraged to refrain from sharing sensationalist content without checking its source.
Privacy & Intellectual Property
Privacy Protection & Data Safetyexpand_more
Your privacy is your right. We urge all users to protect their personal information and respect the privacy of others. Sharing private phone numbers, addresses, or confidential documents—whether your own or someone else's—is prohibited. We recommend users regularly review their privacy settings to control who can see their content.
Respecting Intellectual Propertyexpand_more
We value the hard work of creators. Users must not use images, videos, or written content created by others without obtaining explicit permission. Posting copyrighted material as your own or without authorization may lead to content removal and account penalties. Always give credit where it is due.
Legal & Social Responsibility
Legal Compliance & Cyber Lawsexpand_more
All users must comply with local cyber laws, IT acts, and government guidelines. Any activity that facilitates illegal acts, violates national security, or breaches digital regulations will be reported to the respective law enforcement agencies. We strictly enforce compliance to maintain a lawful and orderly community.
Mindful Sharing & Respectful Interactionexpand_more
Before you post, take a moment to think. We discourage users from sharing content driven by temporary emotions or anger that could lead to regret or conflict. Every user should be respectful of others' opinions, even when they disagree. Fostering a culture of empathy is key to the MingleJoys experience.
Religious & Cyber Integrity
Religious Sanctity & Decencyexpand_more
ZERO TOLERANCE
MingleJoys strictly prohibits mockery or insults directed towards Islam or any religious faith. We do not allow content that spreads misinformation about religious scriptures or promotes ideologies that go against fundamental values. Ridiculing faith in any form will result in an immediate and permanent account termination.
Hacking & Fraud Preventionexpand_more
Any attempt to hack accounts, phish for credentials, or use technical vulnerabilities to exploit users will result in severe consequences. This includes identity theft or creating fake profiles to defraud others. Protecting your digital presence is our highest priority.
