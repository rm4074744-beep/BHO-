General Policies
Content Regulation & Anti-Harassmentexpand_more
MingleJoys strictly regulates all uploaded content to prevent the spread of hate speech, harassment, and cyberbullying. We do not allow any content that targets individuals or groups with the intent to intimidate or silence them.
Combating Fake News & Misinformationexpand_more
Maintaining the truth is essential for a healthy community. We require users to share only verified and accurate information. Intentionally spreading fake news, rumors, or unverified reports is a violation of our standards.
Special Guidelines
Religious Sanctity & Decencyexpand_more
ZERO TOLERANCE
MingleJoys strictly prohibits mockery or insults directed towards Islam or any religious faith. Ridiculing faith results in an immediate and permanent account termination.
